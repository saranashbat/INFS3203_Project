Cybersecurity Analyst

Security Solutions LLC

Job Description

As a Cybersecurity Analyst at Security Solutions LLC, you will play a critical role in protecting our computer systems and networks from cyber threats and attacks. Your responsibilities will include:

  • Implementing and maintaining security measures and protocols to safeguard information systems and data
  • Monitoring network traffic for potential security breaches and investigating security incidents
  • Conducting vulnerability assessments and penetration testing to identify and address security vulnerabilities
  • Developing and implementing security policies and procedures
  • Responding to security incidents and providing incident response support
  • Collaborating with other IT teams to ensure the security of our infrastructure and applications

For more information about Security Solutions LLC, please visit Security Solutions LLC.
